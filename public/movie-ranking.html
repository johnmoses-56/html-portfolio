The Best Movies According to Johnnie

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Longest Ride

"The Longest Ride" is a romantic drama based on Nicholas Sparks' novel, weaving two love stories separated by time. It follows a young couple, Luke and Sophia, whose lives intersect with an elderly man, Ira, as they learn about his decades-long romance with his late wife. The film explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the enduring power of memories.

The Kingdom of Heaven

"Kingdom of Heaven" is an epic historical drama directed by Ridley Scott. Set during the Crusades of the 12th century, it tells the story of Balian of Ibelin, a blacksmith who becomes a knight and defends Jerusalem against the invading forces. The film highlights themes of honor, faith, and the complexities of religious conflict, featuring stunning battle scenes and a rich historical backdrop.

The Shawshank Redemption

"The Shawshank Redemption" is a poignant drama based on Stephen King's novella. It follows the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for the murder of his wife and her lover. Despite his innocence, Andy finds hope and friendship within the prison walls, particularly with fellow inmate Red. The film is a powerful tale of resilience, hope, and the enduring human spirit.
